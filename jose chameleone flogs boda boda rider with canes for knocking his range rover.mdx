Lord mayor contestant and legendary uganda artists jose chameleone yesterday night flogged a boda boda rider for knocking his range rover in makindye.

Boda boda riders are known for recklessness but this one landed on a mad man. Chameleone in the video is seen flogging unidentified boda boda man while some other guy in the background shouts how he he had not knocked him.

After a few canes of struck, chameleone is seen entering his range rover and driving off the scene.

Chameleone is popularly known for causing reckless scenes as bajjo termed him that sometimes he understands while sometimes he is completely mad.
